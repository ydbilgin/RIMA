## STYLE ANCHOR — Her prompta bu kısımı BAŞ'a yapıştır

```
STYLE REFERENCE — MATCH EXACTLY:
Pixel art, dark fantasy. Dead Cells × Hades × Diablo II art direction.
1px black outline. Limited color palette: dark base + ONE energy accent per character.
Battle-worn everywhere: dented armor, torn cloth, scratched weapons. Nothing is pristine.
128x128px canvas. Transparent background. Heroic proportions.
View: low top-down isometric. Character facing southeast (right-forward diagonal).

GLOBAL DO NOT (applies to ALL characters — never violate these):
- Anime, chibi, or exaggerated proportions
- Bright cheerful colors or high fantasy gold trim
- Glowing magical circles floating around character (unless it is a specified class feature)
- Modern or sci-fi elements
- Generic skull/spike aesthetic everywhere
- Rainbow or multi-colored auras
```

## 1. WARBLADE

```
[PASTE STYLE ANCHOR ABOVE THIS]

CHARACTER: WARBLADE — battle-worn heavy warrior.
Partial dark iron plate armor worn over dark leather padding. NOT full plate encasement — chest, arms, legs have plate pieces but gaps show the leather beneath. Every armor plate is DENTED, SCRATCHED, and CRACKED from years of combat. From each crack: COLD BLUE (#7BA7BC) rift energy seeps out like frozen lightning veins trapped in iron. The blue only appears at cracks — it is not a glow around the body.

WEAPON: Two-handed greatsword. The blade is LONGER than the character's full height. Blade surface has cold blue rift energy CRACKS running through it — like lightning frozen into steel. The sword looks impossibly heavy but is held with practiced ease.

FACE: VISIBLE — strong jaw, weathered skin, battle scars, determined grim expression. Either no helmet or a minimal open-face iron nasal helm only. No full face covering.

SILHOUETTE: Aggressive forward lean. Weight low. Body language: "I will close this distance in one second."

CLOAK: Black battle cloak hanging from shoulders. Edges are ragged and torn from combat — not decorative, just functional and damaged.

DO NOT:
- Full plate helmet covering the face (visor, faceplate, great helm)
- Pristine or polished armor — rust, dents, and cracks are required
- Blue magical aura or circles floating around the body
- Purple energy anywhere
- Gold trim, holy symbols, paladin aesthetic
- Pauldrons wider than the character's head
- Living armor feel (no moving armor without a person)
- Glowing blue eyes
- Any armor that looks new, clean, or ceremonial
```

## 4. RANGER ✦ YENİDEN TASARLANMIŞ

```
[PASTE STYLE ANCHOR ABOVE THIS]

CHARACTER: RANGER — tactical rift hunter. She hunts in ruins and dungeons, NOT forests.
DARK CHARCOAL / SLATE GREY leather armor — NO GREEN anywhere on this character. Practical layered leather: chest piece, arm guards, knee-length coat with belt. The aesthetic is dungeon scout, not forest elf.

FACE COVER: Lower face wrapped in a DARK CHARCOAL SCARF — nose and below obscured. Eyes visible. This is practical protection, not a dramatic mask. Hair pulled back tightly.

COWL: LOW TACTICAL COWL — not a large dramatic fantasy hood. A close-fitting cowl that shadows the top of the face slightly. The cowl is the same dark grey as the rest of the armor.

BOW: LONGBOW with COLD BLUE RIFT CRACK ETCHINGS along the wood grain — years of hunting rift creatures have infused the wood with rift energy. The bow itself is dark wood with thin cold blue fracture lines visible along its length. NO large magical glow from the bow — subtle, etched.

ARROWS: Multiple arrow bundles visible on back and at belt. ARROW TIPS have cold blue rift shimmer — sharp and precise.

STANCE: LOW CROUCHING READY STANCE — not standing upright. Weight forward. Always alert. She looks like she's about to move.

NO FUR anywhere. Practical pouches and straps on belt. Lean, functional, zero decoration.

DO NOT:
- Green color anywhere — armor, hood, cloak, or accent
- Large dramatic fantasy hood (Aragorn, Robin Hood, elvish ranger)
- Fur pauldrons or any fur elements
- Elvish or elven visual language
- Long loose flowing hair
- Standing upright at rest (always crouched/ready)
- Purple energy on arrows (cold blue ONLY)
- Forest or nature motifs: leaves, vines, bark texture
- Pristine uninfused bow (the bow MUST have rift crack etchings)
- Full face covering (lower face wrap only — eyes always visible)
```
