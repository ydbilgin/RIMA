Status: BLUEPRINT DRAFT FOR CLAUDE REVIEW
Scope: 30 Act 1 room blueprints before final art/module decisions

## Act 1 Premise

Working production name: `Shattered Keep` until Claude finalizes naming.

Act 1 is not a random cave. It is an old controlled structure that still has readable order:
thresholds, guard halls, cells, chain galleries, reliquaries, shrines, and containment arenas.
The Fracturing damaged this order. Early rooms show the structure; late rooms show the wound.

## Global Act 1 Rules

- Center combat floor stays readable.
- Detail is connected to source anchors, never even random scatter.
- Floor stays quiet; shell/walls carry most visual complexity.
- No giant playable floor padding.
- Camera can include visual shell; movement cannot.
- Doors keep 3+ tile clearance.
- Every combat room keeps at least two dash lanes.
- Elite/boss rooms keep center mostly open.
