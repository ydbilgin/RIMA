```text

Hard requirements:

- same camera angle as the reference image
- same scale as the reference image
- no zoom-in
- dark fantasy ARPG character concept for gameplay readability
- grounded proportions, not anime, not chibi
- partial armor over cloth unless explicitly stated otherwise
- battle-worn materials, worn leather, torn cloth, dented metal
- clean silhouette, readable weapon shape, readable hand pose
- one clear accent color system only when specified
- black or near-black background

Do NOT do:
- no bright high fantasy gold plate
- no generic rainbow magic
- no extra floating props unless specified
- no painterly blur
- no soft mushy edges
- no front-facing portrait pose
- no cute style
- no oversized head or short limbs
```
