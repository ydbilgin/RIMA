---

Pixel art character roster sheet for a 2D action roguelite game called RIMA.

Reference image attached: use this as the target art style — same pixel density, same outline weight, same rendering technique, same atmospheric dark fantasy tone. Reproduce this visual quality and consistency across all 10 characters.

LAYOUT: 10 characters, 2 rows of 5, each character in a 165×165px cell, drawn at 128px tall.
Total sheet approx 1650×330px, pure black background. No text labels, no UI, no floor, no environment, no shadows.

CAMERA: 30–35° low top-down ARPG gameplay view, characters facing SOUTH or slight natural SW idle angle. Full body visible — top of head, shoulder planes, top of boots, mild face foreshortening. Same angle for all 10. NO eye-level portrait, NO side-view, NO heroic poster angle.

STYLE:
- All 10 drawn by the same artist — identical outline thickness, identical painterly weathered pixel shading, identical sprite scale
- Mature proportions: 7-head body ratio. NOT chibi, NOT anime, NOT oversized heads
- Reference games: Diablo 2 × Hades × Hero Siege × Last Epoch — readability over detail
- Atmospheric dark fantasy, NOT grimdark, NOT near-black overall
- Muted readable base palette, one signature accent color per character
- Practical combat fantasy gear: field-repaired materials, asymmetry, scars, ritual marks
- Characters are grounded human adventurers after the Fracturing: worn, capable, iconic — not superheroes, not generic NPCs
- RIFT RESIDUE IS SUBTLE: tiny glowing cracks, dim rift residue in wounds/weapon edges only. No big elemental glow, no aura, no smoke clouds, no active spell effects — those are VFX layer, not base sprite
- FACES: 10 visually distinct faces — different face shapes, hair colors/styles, expressions. Zero copy-paste face templates

[02] ELEMENTALIST — Field Arcanist [Female]
