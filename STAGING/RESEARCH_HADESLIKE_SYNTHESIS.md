## 3. FLOATING-ROOM DESIGN PASS (the "no walls" question)

**Verdict: a strong room design is fully achievable on the existing floating-island system — walls are explicitly OPTIONAL.** NLM canon LOCKS "wall-less floating-island, Hades Elysium; boundary = cliff + sparse column + cyan rune-circle + brazier," and the locked cliff system already drops faces into the void. The job is **edge-framing and depth**, not building enclosures. The genre reference (Hades Elysium / Asphodel) proves a void-edged arena reads as a complete combat space without walls.

**Concrete pass (all on existing systems, no new walls):**
1. **Edge frame = broken-masonry rim + cliff (already locked).** Keep the 3-direction cliff (S/SE/SW) dropping into void; add a low **broken-masonry rim** silhouette on the back/far edges so the eye reads a boundary without a solid wall. This satisfies canon's "camera framing should not show raw black emptiness around the player."
2. **Vertical punctuation = sparse columns + chains + braziers** at the rim (canon boundary kit). Columns give Y-sort depth and parallax; hanging chains sell the "shattered keep suspended over void"; braziers (already retuned cyan per lighting-lock) light the perimeter. Keep them **sparse** (canon) — frame, don't clutter the playable floor.
3. **Void depth = parallax backdrop** (RoomBackgroundRig nebula already live) + floating shards drifting below the rim. This is what *replaces* the back wall: the player feels held over depth instead of boxed in.
4. **Focal rift-prop** at the room's visual anchor (`RoomSequenceData.focalElementPrefab` already exists) — a cyan seal-rift that is the room's identity and the eventual hook for the Brink-reacts escalation (#3) and reward identity (cx #15: altar rooms bias passives, chain-bridge rooms bias actives).
5. **Per-room cyan-leak escalation** — earliest rooms calm/dim, deeper rooms leak progressively more cyan from cracks/rift (room-fill canon supports this; ties directly to converged idea #3). This makes the 5-room arc *read* as a tightening seal even before #3's mechanical layer exists.

**Walls = mark OPTIONAL/LATER.** Canon has `WallCollisionMask` + 64×96 wall modules in the *production art-direction backlog* (for interior/crypt rooms), but they are staging, not required for the floating demo. Recommendation: **do the edge-framing pass now (cheap, on-identity); defer authored wall modules to a later "interior biome" if ever.** The demo's 5 floating rooms should ship framed-by-void, not walled.
