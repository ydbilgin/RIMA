# RIMA — ChatGPT Image Generation Prompt (Wall-less Gameplay Concept Art)

**Usage:** Paste the prompt below into ChatGPT (with image generation enabled — GPT-4o / ChatGPT Plus). Ask for 10 images. ChatGPT'ye yaratıcılık özgürlüğü bırakılmış — her varyasyonu kendi seçer.

---

## PROMPT TO PASTE

---

You are designing concept art for **RIMA**, a 2D top-down pixel art action roguelike (ARPG) set in a dark fantasy world of broken seals and leaking rift energy.

I want you to generate **10 distinct gameplay concept art images** (16:9, ~1024×576 each) showing different scenes from the game. The visual direction is locked, but I give you **creative freedom on composition, framing, room layout, lighting nuance, and which character/situation to highlight per image**. Mix combat, exploration, boss encounter, and ambient moments across the 10 images. Each image should feel like a polished gameplay screenshot or PR key art, not concept sketches.

---

### LOCKED VISUAL DIRECTION (Hades-Elysium meets Hyper Light Drifter)

- **Perspective:** High top-down 3/4 (~70-80° from horizon). Think Hades, Diablo III, Children of Morta. Tiles slightly foreshortened, characters readable from above.
- **Art style:** Pixel art with painterly shading. Hard pixel edges, hand-pixeled detail, NO anti-aliasing, NO smooth gradients.
- **Wall-less design:** NO traditional walls. Boundary is established by:
  - Stone floor tiles ending at **cliff edges** (drop-off into void below)
  - Sparse standalone **broken stone columns** (2-3 tile tall, weathered)
  - **Cyan rune circles** glowing on the floor (focal points, runic energy)
  - **Brazier with warm orange flame** (warm light pool, defining boundary corners)
  - Subtle **floating island** silhouette — the arena IS the island
- **Color palette:**
  - Floor: dark slate gray (#3A3D42), weathered cobblestone
  - Cool accent: cyan rift glow (#00FFCC), rune circles, energy seams
  - Warm accent: orange brazier fire (#E89020), rim light on player
  - Void: deep black with subtle particle drift (motes, dust, embers)
- **Lighting:** Dramatic, low-key. Dim ambient + 2-3 focal lights (cyan rune pulse + warm brazier + subtle moon/sky from above). High contrast between lit zone and dark void around the arena.

### DEPTH-DOWNWARD (very important)
- Show that the arena is suspended above a void — the camera should suggest there's **deep darkness BELOW the floor tiles**
- Cliff edges should have **drop shadow** falling into void
- Optional: **parallax background layers** visible past the cliff (distant cathedral ruin, far rift glow, drifting fog)
- 2-3 of the 10 images should explicitly show the **floating island shape** from a slight side-tilted angle to emphasize depth

### WALL-LESS DOORS / TRANSITIONS (use 2-3 of these in your 10 images)
Since there are no walls, exits between rooms use:
1. **Stone bridges** spanning the void to the next island
2. **Cyan rift portals** (standalone glowing archway, no surrounding wall)
3. **Cliff descent / ascent** (stair-edge to lower or higher floating island)
4. **Light gates** (vertical energy threshold, no physical structure)

### ANIMATED BACKGROUND MOODS (suggest 1 per image)
Show the bg behind/below the arena reflecting the room's mood:
- **Combat arena** → cyan particle drift, occasional energy ripple in void
- **Boss arena** → heavy embers, lightning flashes deep below
- **Treasure / safe room** → warm amber glow pulse, slow dust drift
- **Ritual room** → mist + glyphs fading in/out, cool blue light from below
- **Transition corridor** → calm parallax of distant ruins

---

### THE 4 PLAYER CHARACTERS (chibi pixel art, ~2.5 heads tall, large head over short sturdy body — feature at least 3 of the 4 across your 10 images)

1. **Warblade** (male, melee, tank-warrior)
   - Warm tan skin, tousled dark brown-to-black hair, no helmet, stern focused expression
   - Heavy dark gunmetal + black plate armor, silver studs, leather straps, bracers, heavy boots
   - Carries a **large two-handed steel greatsword** with brown grip
   - Palette: black, charcoal, dark steel, muted brown leather, small bright metal highlights
   - Stocky, battle-ready silhouette — NOT tall or elegant

2. **Ranger** (female, ranged, hunter)
   - Light skin, **long pale blonde hair** falling around shoulders, lively alert expression
   - Dark teal + slate + leather hunter clothing, shoulder wraps, fitted boots, practical straps
   - Carries a **longbow** (always visible — NOT a crossbow or firearm)
   - Slim and agile, wilderness scout silhouette
   - Palette: blonde hair, dark teal fabric, charcoal leather, small cool metal details

3. **Shadowblade** (male, melee, stealth assassin)
   - Medium tan skin, short black hair, dark brows, narrow eyes, severe stealthy expression
   - **Black + dark gray bodysuit** with angular armor plates, small muted **crimson red** accents, gloves, boots
   - Wields **dual short daggers** (sharp, visible)
   - Slim and fast, masked-assassin feel
   - Palette: black, charcoal, muted steel, tiny red accents

4. **Elementalist** (female, ranged, magic caster)
   - Fair skin, **short orange hair swept to one side**, soft features, calm bright expression
   - Practical **teal + blue mage outfit** — light shirt, darker skirt/robe panels, boots, simple gloves
   - Weapon = **magical orb / spell light in hand** (no staff, no dark necromancer vibe)
   - Petite youthful adult, neat readable silhouette
   - Palette: warm orange hair, teal-blue caster palette, glowing cyan/white spell light

### THE BOSS — PENITENT SOVEREIGN (Act 1 boss, feature in 2 of the 10 images)

- **Lore:** Former guardian of the Shattered Keep. Imprisoned rift energy within himself as self-punishment. Bound by chains he forged himself.
- **Scale:** **3-4x player size**. Massive 256×256 sprite equivalent. Hulking presence.
- **Phase 1 silhouette:** Heavy hunched figure, bound by his own thick chains, kneeling or stooped posture. Tarnished armor, ritualistic plating. **Purple + cyan rift light spills from his chest** through armor cracks.
- **Phase 2 transformation:** Chains BREAK and float around him. Stands upright. Speed and aggression increase. Surrounded by a permanent **3m cyan Rift Tear** at the arena center.
- **Phase 3:** Summons **Echo Phantoms** (ghostly purple/cyan duplicates) around him. Arena becomes chaos of shifting hazard zones.
- **Boss arena:** Larger floating island (40×30 cells), more cliff edges, central cyan Rift Tear hazard, broken monument wall in distant bg (off-island, parallax), heavy embers + lightning in void below

### THE 10 IMAGES — your creative freedom

Generate 10 distinct scenes that **showcase the wall-less HYBRID Hades-Elysium style of RIMA**. Each image is independent. Suggested mix (you can deviate creatively):

1. **Hero shot — Warblade entering arena** (sword raised, sparse columns, cliff edges visible, cyan runes glowing)
2. **Combat moment — Ranger mid-shot** (drawing bow, arrow trail, against 2-3 skeletal enemies, brazier light pool)
3. **Stealth — Shadowblade dashing** (motion trail, low light, dagger flash, between columns)
4. **Magic burst — Elementalist channeling** (cyan/white spell circle in hand, energy radiating, on rune cluster)
5. **Bridge transition** (stone bridge spans void between two islands, hero crossing, deep void below visible)
6. **Rift portal** (standalone glowing cyan arch on island edge, hero approaching, no walls anywhere)
7. **Boss reveal — Penitent Sovereign Phase 1** (chains, kneeling, rift light bleeding from chest, hero in foreground tiny by comparison)
8. **Boss fight — Penitent Sovereign Phase 2** (chains broken, central Rift Tear hazard, hero dashing, lightning in void)
9. **Treasure / safe moment** (warm brazier glow, hero resting near altar/chest, calm mood, amber drift particles)
10. **Atmospheric wide shot** (no character, pure environment — floating island with cliff edges, parallax ruins in distant void, full mood study)

---

### CRITICAL "DO NOT" LIST
- ❌ NO traditional perimeter walls boxing the arena
- ❌ NO cartoon outline / no anime style / no painterly soft gradients
- ❌ NO bright saturated colors except the cyan rift + warm brazier accents
- ❌ NO modern UI overlay (HUD bars, menus) in these — keep them pure gameplay scenes
- ❌ NO ground-level cliff-side perspective — keep the HIGH top-down view
- ❌ NO grass / forest / outdoor naturalism — this is a haunted shattered keep on a void-suspended island

### CRITICAL "DO" LIST
- ✅ DO emphasize the **wall-less premium look** — that's what makes RIMA visually distinct
- ✅ DO show **depth into void** below the arena
- ✅ DO use **cyan rift + warm brazier** contrast as core lighting language
- ✅ DO render in **pixel art** with hard edges
- ✅ DO mix character/combat/ambient/boss scenes for variety
- ✅ DO let your creative interpretation shine — RIMA's visual language is a starting point, give me your best premium interpretation

---

Generate all 10 images. Number each one (1-10) and add a one-sentence caption describing what's happening.
