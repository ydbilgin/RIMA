### **Summary of Research & Adaptation Design**

Here is a quick overview of the top recommendations mapping to RIMA's mechanics:

*   **Rally Mechanic ([Dead Cells]):** Taking damage retains a fading cyan "Seal Integrity" line. Landing a **Commit-Beat** or executing a **Sundered Beat** (BREAK -> EXECUTE) on a **Broken/Sundered** enemy recovers this health. (Low Implementation Cost)
*   **Omega Charging ([Hades 2]):** Active skills can be held down to spend class resource (e.g., Rage) to trigger enhanced "Sundered" variants (e.g., Warblade's `Iron Charge` hold -> `Blade Rush` mode to immediately break guard). (Low Implementation Cost)
*   **Bullet-Clearing Blanks ([Enter the Gungeon]):** Executing a low-health/Broken enemy triggers a "Seal Shatter" shockwave that clears screen projectiles and staggers nearby units. (Low Implementation Cost)
*   **Cast Lodging ([Hades 1]):** Lodging a **Seal Shard** inside targets to reduce their guard threshold, which can then be detonate-shattered by landing a basic attack **Commit-Beat**. (Medium Implementation Cost)
*   **Erosion Limit ([Cinderia]):** Shifting classes or using high-power skills builds a **Seal Erosion** meter that triggers randomized **Void Anomaly** curses at 100%. (Medium Implementation Cost)
