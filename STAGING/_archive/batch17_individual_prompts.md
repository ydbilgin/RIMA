## 01 — WARBLADE

```
64x64 pixel art chibi character sprite, male.
Into Samomor palette reference.

FACING: True south. Direct front view, perfectly symmetric.
Left and right shoulders MUST BE equal width and perfectly parallel to the screen.
NO south-east, NO south-west, NO three-quarter rotation. Spine is perfectly vertical.
High top-down 35 degree camera angle, face fully visible.

Warblade heavy warrior:
Broad torso, forward-leaning posture, shoulders pushed forward,
head slightly lowered while face remains readable.
Low center of gravity, heavy grounded stance.
Legs appear foreshortened (shorter) due to top-down camera.
Both feet must point straight down to the bottom of the canvas.

ONE single greatsword — one blade, one hilt. NOT dual wielding. NOT two swords.
This is a two-handed weapon: the character holds the single hilt with BOTH hands together,
right hand above left, as if gripping a baseball bat with two hands on the same handle.
The sword hangs in a low guard — hilt at waist, single blade angled down toward the ground,
tip pointing to the lower-right. The blade runs diagonally in front of the lower body.
Sword always held in both hands, never sheathed, never on back.

Dark battle-worn leather armor with minimal plate reinforcements.
Brass buckle accents at chest and belt. No ornate decoration.
Short dark hair, square jaw. No helmet. No cape.

Muted desaturated palette. Dark warm brown-grey body, weathered brass accents, cool steel blade.
Thick black outline. Hard pixel edges. No anti-aliasing.
85-90 percent canvas fill. Transparent background.
```
