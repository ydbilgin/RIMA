Confirm/deny on `feedback_state_vs_n_frames_cost_lock`:
- Confirm directionally: character_state is materially more expensive than V3 `n_frames` animation and should not be used casually.
- The exact "4-8x" ratio is not universal:
  - vs one 6-8f direction: 20-40 vs 2 gen = 10-20x.
  - vs 5-dir 6-8f pass: 20-40 vs 10 gen = 2-4x.
  - vs 8-dir 6-8f pass: 20-40 vs 16 gen = 1.25-2.5x.
- Practical rule: use `n_frames`/V3 for normal animation; use `character_state` only when split-pipeline apex pose lock is required.
