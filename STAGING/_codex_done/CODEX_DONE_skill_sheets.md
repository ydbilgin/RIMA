## Sheet Verdicts

- Warblade: clean and readable. Iron/orange combat identity reads strongly; gravity cleave and earthbreak icons are clear.
- Ronin: readable, with strong sakura/katana identity. Some generated internal labels are busy, but deterministic overlay labels make the sheet usable.
- Gunslinger: clean and one of the strongest concept sheets. Dual pistol, reticle, bullet-time, and teal/gold identity read instantly.
- Ranger: clean and readable. Trap/mark/arrow fantasy is clear and fits the forest hunter palette.
- Elementalist: strongest visual skill set overall. Fire, ice, lightning, prism, and teleport icons are highly distinct and immediately readable.
- Shadowblade: clean and readable. Teal/red/poison/shadow identity is strong; assassin silhouette works well.
- Ravager: readable and high impact. Rage/axe/whirlwind visuals are strong, though the palette is heavier and darker.
- Hexer: readable and coherent. Cursemark, plague hand, and soul-drain icons align well with decay/DoT identity.
- Brawler: clean and readable. Punch/grapple/impact icons are simple and instantly legible.
- Summoner: clean and readable. Spirit/minion control identity is clear, with strong cyan ghost-white cohesion.

## Strongest Visual Skill Set

Elementalist is the strongest visually. The six selected skills naturally create separate icon silhouettes and colors: Fireball, Frozen Orb, Prism Beam, Chain Lightning, Meteor, and Blink.

## Concept Skill Recommendations

- Gunslinger must-keep: Twin Fire, Ricochet Shot, Bullet Time, Dead Eye. Drop or merge: Powder Burst can merge into Smoke Round; Fan The Hammer overlaps Twin Fire unless treated as ultimate burst.
- Ravager must-keep: Berserk, Axe Throw, Earthcrack, Whirlwind, Bloodthirst. Drop or merge: War Cry and Crimson Roar overlap; keep Crimson Roar if the class needs an ultimate identity button.
- Hexer must-keep: Curse Mark, Decay Aura, Hex Bolt, Soul Drain, Plague Touch. Drop or merge: Necrosis overlaps Decay Aura unless Necrosis is the execute; Death Wail should be reserved for elite/ultimate tier.
- Brawler must-keep: Jab, Cross, Uppercut, Body Lock, Knockout. Drop or merge: Power Strike overlaps Cross; Iron Stance is useful only if defensive stance is part of the kit.
- Summoner must-keep: Summon Wisp, Spirit Bind, Soul Link, Ethereal Guard, Familiar Strike. Drop or merge: Pact Drain overlaps Sacrifice; Beacon is useful only if minion positioning becomes a core mechanic.
