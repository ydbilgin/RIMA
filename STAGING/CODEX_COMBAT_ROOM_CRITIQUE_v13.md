# v13 Combat Critique

## Why v13 reads as a ritual chamber
- The center is dominated by a gold ritual sigil under the player, making the room feel ceremonial rather than tactical.
- Two center braziers and a north brazier frame the sigil like a shrine setup instead of giving the player a clean dodge lane.
- The east portal puddle is a cool blue objective focal point, which reads as a mystical destination rather than a combat threat.
- Moss creep, candelabra, hanging chain, banner, and scattered pebbles create decorative ambience without encounter purpose.
- The central columns sit too close to the player focus, so the readable combat lane is muddied by shrine props.

## What needs to go
- Remove the ritual sigil from the center.
- Remove or move peaceful candelabra and shrine-like brazier clustering out of the combat center.
- Remove the portal puddle as the objective/focal element.
- Remove moss/garden creep and excessive breadcrumb pebbles that read as scenic dressing.
- Remove any treasure/reward implication; no chest, loot pile, or reward marker should be visible.

## What needs to come in
- A clear 6x6 open center around x=15..21, y=8..14 for movement and dodging.
- Perimeter cover at NW, NE, SW, and SE using columns, broken walls, debris, and a defeated statue.
- A north arched doorway using the v3 `wall_11` arched wall to establish the room exit.
- Five implied enemy spawn markers as ash/blood/scorch decals around the perimeter, with no actual enemies spawned.
- A single center threat mark, not a ritual symbol: small scorch/rift crack only.
- Cooler ambient light, one warm red/orange threat light, and heavy edge vignette.
