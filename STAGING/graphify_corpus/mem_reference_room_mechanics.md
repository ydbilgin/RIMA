# PROGRESSION (ROOM COUNTS)
* Act 1: 8 - 9 rooms
* Act 2 & 3: 9 - 11 rooms
* Final: 5 - 6 rooms

# CORE SYSTEMS
* Echo Imprint: 1 reward every 3 combat rooms. Max 4 per run.
* Tag Synergy: 2 same tags = Auto-passive activation. Max 2 active.
* Skill Draft: 3 options post-combat/elite. Elite rewards are Rare+.

# HP ECONOMY
* Rest Rooms: NONE.
* Recovery: Shop (30/70 Gold), Boss Kill (50%), Curse Gate (5%), Post-Boss Act Transition (50%).
* Elite Recovery: 12% on kill.
