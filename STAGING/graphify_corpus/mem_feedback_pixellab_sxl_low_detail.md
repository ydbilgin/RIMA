---
name: PixelLab S-XL Low Detail Default
description: Use "Low detailed" for 64px character sprites in S-XL.
type: feedback
---
* **Rule:** Detail = **Low detailed** (S-XL Dropdown).
* **Reason:** Highly detailed creates noise when downsampling to 64px.
* **View:** **Low top-down** (30-35° 3/4). **High top-down BANNED** (extreme angle kills identity).
* **Settings:** 256x256 -> Downsample to 64px (Aseprite nearest-neighbor).
* **Exception:** 128px+ (Bosses/Key Art) can use Highly detailed.
