* **Rule:** **Combat-Practical Exposed** (Sleeveless, cropped, asymmetric).
* **Prohibition:** Full closed robes/coats, bikini armor, lingerie, underboob, superhero catsuits.
* **Reason:** Forearm/chest exposure required for class signatures (tattoos/scars/marks).
* **Standards:**
  - **Elementalist:** Sleeveless asymmetric tunic, cropped vest, exposed forearms (burns/frost).
  - **Ranger:** Cropped chest wrap, midriff partial (straps), sleeveless.
  - **Gunslinger:** Open knee-length asymmetric coat, exposed sternum/forearms.
  - **Hexer:** Cropped hood, asymmetric ritual tunic, exposed forearms (curse tattoos).
* **Guideline:** Functional exposure (Story delivery) != Sexual objectification.
