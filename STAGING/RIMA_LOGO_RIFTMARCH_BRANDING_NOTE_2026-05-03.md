# RIMA Logo / Rift March Branding Note - 2026-05-03

## Current User Direction

The game name reads as `RIMA`, but the buried/expanded phrase is `Rift March`.

Desired creative split:
- Main read: `RI MA`
- Hidden/broken secondary read:
  - `RI'ft`
  - `MA'rch`

The logo should make `RI MA` immediately readable, while the fractured secondary read hints at
`Rift March` through cracks, broken fragments, and small displaced letters.

Reference image provided by user:
- Large cracked stone/metal `RIMA` wordmark with gold rift cracks and warm glow on black.

## Current Image Verdict

Strong:
- The stone/metal mass works for RIMA.
- Gold cracks communicate rift energy clearly.
- Black background and glow fit the dark fantasy/dungeon identity.

Needs improvement:
- It reads only as `RIMA`; the `Rift March` idea is not yet visible.
- Glow is too even behind the whole word, so the rift energy loses hierarchy.
- Cracks are visually exciting but too uniformly distributed.
- The `RI` / `MA` split is not emphasized enough.
- Small-title usability is unknown; the current detail may collapse at UI sizes.

## Better Logo Direction

Keep the main logo as a heavy fractured stone/iron wordmark, but structure it around a clear
central rupture:

- Separate `RI` and `MA` with a deliberate vertical rift gap.
- Make the central rift the brightest point, not every crack.
- Let some crack paths create or reveal small hidden letters:
  - `ft` appears as broken gold/stone fragments falling from or beside `RI`.
  - `rch` appears as fractured under-text or fallen fragments under/after `MA`.
- Do not force full subtitle readability at the cost of the main logo. First read must always be
  `RIMA`.

Suggested composition:
- Top/main: `RI   MA`
- Between `I` and `M`: bright vertical rift fracture.
- Under `RI`: small broken `FT` fragments, partly separated by cracks.
- Under `MA`: small broken `RCH` fragments, like collapsed marching-stone pieces.
- Optional full subtle secondary line for larger title screens only: `RIFT MARCH`, carved faintly
  under the main logo, with the `RI` and `MA` letters visually linked to the main title.

## Art Rules

- Use 2-3 value layers in the stone, not noisy full-detail everywhere.
- Gold rift lines should have hierarchy:
  - 1 primary bright rift
  - 3-5 secondary cracks
  - small hairline cracks only where needed
- Outer glow should be localized around rifts and silhouette edges, not a uniform rectangle.
- Keep black negative space behind the logo.
- Preserve a clean silhouette for small sizes.
- Make a no-glow or reduced-glow variant for UI/header use.

## Deliverables To Make Later

- Full title logo: wide, high detail, black/transparent background variants.
- Compact menu logo: less glow, fewer cracks, readable at smaller size.
- Icon/mark: central `RI|MA` rift or just the cracked `R`.
- Monochrome/flat version for docs, loading marks, and UI embossing.
