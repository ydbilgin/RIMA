R2. OPEN VISTA - Karar #61 EDGE
- Acceptable as authored pocket within the macro graph, not open world, if it is optional, template-authored, small, finite, and exits back to the same node.
- It crosses the line if pockets have branching routes, persistent spatial continuity, free-roam discovery, required progression, or more than one reward-critical objective.
- 20x40 is large for RIMA, but still room-scale if it has one entrance, one exit, one encounter beat, and no overworld navigation loop.

R3. MOB SKILL OVERLOAD
- Full T1+T2+T3 on every mob is too much for readability. T3 pair combos especially risk invisible causality unless the room composition is deliberately built around them.
- Rule: normal rooms should allow max 2 active T2-capable mobs at once, and max 1 T3 combo family per room. Elite rooms may allow 3 T2-capable mobs and 1 T3 combo. Boss add phases should disable T3.
- With an 8-12 threat budget, cap simultaneous T2 activations to 2 in normal rooms, 3 in elite rooms, and 1 during boss pressure windows. Anything above that breaks the 1.5s tell / 0.4s response floor.
- Several T2s need cooldown gates: Tear Open, Seal Repair Channel, Imp Tide, and Subterranean Web can multiply threat without adding clear telegraphs.

R4. BOSS ENV HAZARD TIMING
- Phase 2 overlap is a real fairness risk. Existing Rift Tear plus new Rift Bloom both occupy terrain, apply slow/damage, and narrow the arena.
- A 40x30 arena can support both only if they are the same hazard language or mutually scheduled. Do not run independent timers.
- Recommended: merge Rift Bloom into Rift Tear as the phase escalation. Example: Rift Tear opens cracks; every 8s one crack blooms for 2s after a clear tell. Maintain at least 55-60% traversable floor, no bloom during 3x Fracture Strike startup, and no slow stacking.

R5. PRODUCTION SCOPE
- Combined Faz 1-2 scope is not realistic alongside 16 sprite batch and 4 boss design.
- Room Designer E1-E2 is realistic and directly attacks the current bottleneck.
- Open Vista is viable only as visual polish: one F1 parallax kit plus boss backdrop in Faz 1; defer 3 pocket templates and multi-floor vistas.
- Mob Skill Expansion is the largest risk. 12-15 VFX, 8-12 animations, behavior tree changes, and pair detection is a full combat-system expansion, not a small content pass.
- Cut plan: Faz 1 gets T2 for 3 mobs only, no T3, and one boss hazard merge. Faz 2 gets remaining T2. Faz 3 gets T3 if combat readability is proven.

R6. MISSING ITEMS
- Open Vista missing room exclusions: Shop, Rest, Entry, and Boss should not have optional pockets. Mystery and Curse Gate are correct candidates; Elite can have vista dressing but no side-pocket reward path in Faz 1.
- Open Vista missing requirement: pocket reward table must be non-progression-critical and capped per run.
- Mob Skills missing role: no true summoner/minion-spam stress test beyond Relic Caster and Void Thrall. Add one controlled summoner test room, not a new mob yet.
- Mob Skills missing tooling: per-room threat tags and cooldown arbitration. Without a director rule, pair synergies will produce accidental spikes.
- Room Designer missing critical workflow gap: tile validation report. The wizard should flag wrong PPU, non-32 multiples, compression/mipmap/filter errors, missing collision metadata, bad naming, and duplicate IDs before import.

R7. CUT ONE / RANK 3
- Cut for Faz 1: Mob/Boss Skill Expansion. It touches combat readability, AI, VFX, animation, encounter composition, and boss fairness simultaneously.
- Rank by player-experience leverage:
- 1. Room Designer Top-Down Enhancement: fastest path to more rooms, better iteration, fewer tile mistakes.
- 2. Open Vista Integration: strong identity gain, especially for Fractured Epic tone, if limited to backdrop/parallax.
- 3. Mob/Boss Skill Expansion: high eventual value, but too risky before base enemies, sprites, rooms, and first boss are stable.

R8. ALTERNATIVES
- Open Vista cheaper 80/30: make static vista windows and wall/floor rift decals first. One parallax layer only in boss rooms and Mystery/Curse rooms. No playable pockets in Faz 1.

- Boss hazard cheaper 80/30: reskin and schedule existing Rift Tear variants instead of adding independent hazard systems.
- Room Designer cheaper 80/30: do E1 plus validation first. E2 can be a preview mode overlay, not a full Pixel Perfect Camera simulation. E5 dry-run can wait.

R9. NEW DECISIONS
- Karar #83: Optional side-pockets are authored sub-rooms only. They do not count as macro-map nodes, cannot be required for boss access, cannot appear in Shop/Rest/Entry, and are capped at 2 per run until proven.
- Karar #84: Enemy expansion budget is staged. Faz 1 normal rooms may use max 2 simultaneous T2-capable enemies, Elite max 3, and T3 pair synergies are forbidden until the base Faz 1 combat readability pass is approved.

TLDR:
- Top approval: Room Designer E1-E2 is strongest as-is.
- Top concern: Mob skill expansion overloads readability and production at the same time.
- Top cut recommendation: Defer T3 and most T2 mob expansion to Faz 2+; keep only 3 T2 prototypes in Faz 1.
- Top missing item: Tile import validation/reporting in the Room Designer workflow.
