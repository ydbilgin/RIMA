# Canonical Sprite Bible - Skill Sheets v6

## Warblade
Warblade is a young adult chibi male warrior with a large head over a short, sturdy body, roughly 2.5 heads tall. He has warm tan skin, tousled dark brown to black hair, heavy brows, and a stern focused expression. His silhouette is armored but compact, with dark gunmetal and black plate pieces, silver studs, leather straps, bracers, and heavy boots. The visible weapon identity is a large two-handed steel greatsword with a brown grip, held close to the body in the south view. His body type reads stocky and battle-ready rather than tall or elegant. The palette is mostly black, charcoal, dark steel, and muted brown leather, with small bright metal highlights. His signature is the rugged heavy armor plus oversized greatsword, with no helmet, no cape, and no ornate fantasy exaggeration.

## Ronin
Ronin is a young adult chibi male swordsman with a large head, short body, slim build, and calm upright stance. He has light warm skin, sharp dark eyebrows, narrow eyes, and black hair pulled into a neat topknot or swept-back samurai style. His expression is controlled and serious, suggesting discipline rather than rage. He wears dark navy and charcoal layered robes with a small warm sash or belt detail, loose sleeves, and simple sandals or dark footwear. His katana identity is visible at the waist and should stay clean, narrow, and elegant. The silhouette is spare and vertical, with no bulky armor and no excessive cloth volume. His visual signature is the restrained dark robe, tied hair, and poised katana-draw posture.

## Gunslinger
Gunslinger is a young adult chibi female gunfighter with dark brown skin, a large head, slim compact body, and a confident forward-facing stance. She has very dark brown to black hair styled in textured locks or short layered strands around the head, with a sharp, cool expression. Her outfit is a dark rift-tech coat or long jacket with black, gray, and pale metallic accents over fitted combat clothing. Twin pistols are the class weapon identity and should remain clearly visible in both hands or at the hips. Her build reads agile and lean rather than armored or heavy. The palette is dominated by black, charcoal, muted steel, and small cool highlights, with her skin tone providing strong contrast. Her signature is the dual-pistol silhouette, dark longcoat, and sleek modern-fantasy duelist attitude.

## Ranger
Ranger is a young adult chibi female archer with light skin, a large head, short athletic body, and a lively hunter stance. She has long pale blonde hair that frames the face and falls around the shoulders, with a confident alert expression. Her outfit uses dark teal, slate, and leather hunter clothing with shoulder wraps, fitted boots, and practical straps. The longbow is the core weapon identity and must stay visible, not replaced by a crossbow or firearm. Her build is slim and agile, with a wilderness scout silhouette rather than a mage or knight silhouette. The palette mixes blonde hair, dark teal fabric, charcoal leather, and small cool metal details. Her signature is the bright blonde hair against dark ranger gear and the readable bow-and-arrow profile.

## Elementalist
Elementalist is a young adult chibi female mage with fair skin, a large head, very small body, and a gentle upright posture. She has short to medium orange hair swept to one side, soft facial features, and a calm, bright expression. Her clothing is a practical teal and blue mage outfit with a light shirt, darker skirt or robe panels, boots, and simple gloves or wrist details. She should read as a clean elemental caster, not a robed necromancer or heavily armored spellblade. The weapon identity is magical focus energy or an orb-staff motif, with hands and spell light kept visible. Her build is petite and youthful but adult, with a neat readable silhouette. Her signature is the warm orange hair, teal-blue caster palette, and clean elemental magic identity.

## Shadowblade
Shadowblade is a young adult chibi male assassin with medium tan skin, large head, short lean body, and a tight guarded stance. He has short black hair, dark brows, narrow eyes, and a severe expression that reads stealthy and controlled. His outfit is a black and dark gray bodysuit with angular armor plates, small red or muted crimson accents, gloves, boots, and a compact tactical silhouette. Dual daggers are the class weapon identity and should remain sharp, short, and visible. His build is slim and fast rather than bulky, with minimal cloth and no bright heroic palette. The color signature is black, charcoal, muted steel, and tiny red accents. His visual signature is the masked-assassin feel, dark armor suit, and paired dagger silhouette.

## Ravager
Ravager is a young adult chibi male berserker with tan skin, a large head, short powerful body, and broad muscular shoulders. He has long dark brown hair, a fierce scowling face, thick brows, and a rugged battle-worn expression. His outfit is barbarian armor with brown leather, dark red cloth, metal bracers, heavy boots, and exposed muscular arms. The weapon identity is a huge axe, which should stay oversized, brutal, and readable in action poses. His body type is the heaviest of the roster, compact but muscular. The palette is warm brown leather, dark red rage accents, black hair, and dull steel. His signature is the wild hair, exposed strength, red-brown berserker palette, and massive axe.

## Hexer
Hexer is a young adult chibi female curse caster with pale gray-lavender skin, a large head, small narrow body, and a still ominous stance. She has long dark blue-black or purple-black hair framing the face, cool pale eyes, and an unreadable haunted expression. Her clothing is a dark hooded or high-collar robe in black, deep navy, and violet tones, with small silver or cursed trinket details. The weapon identity is cursed magic, a totem, charm, or small occult focus rather than a staff-heavy wizard silhouette. Her build is slim and eerie, with a compact cloak shape and minimal exposed skin. The palette is cold black, violet, muted blue, and pale gray skin. Her signature is the pale face inside dark hair and robes, with cursed trinkets and purple-green hex energy.

## Brawler
Brawler is a young adult chibi male fist fighter with dark brown skin, a large head, short muscular body, and squared fighting stance. He has close-cropped black hair, strong eyebrows, and a focused aggressive expression. His outfit is minimal melee gear: brown wraps, dark pants, belt details, boots, and reinforced hand wraps or gauntlets. The class weapon identity is fists or gauntlets, never blades, guns, or magic staves. His body type is compact, athletic, and muscular, with broad arms and a grounded boxer silhouette. The palette is warm skin, dark brown leather, muted gold or brass wrap accents, and charcoal cloth. His signature is the bare-arm punch silhouette, gauntlet/wrap hands, and direct hand-to-hand combat posture.

## Summoner
Summoner is a young adult chibi female occult caster with very pale skin, a large head, small body, and a composed ritual stance. She has long dark teal-black hair, cool blue-green facial tones, and a quiet serious expression. Her outfit is a teal and dark blue long coat or robe with pale trim, layered sleeves, and a slim vertical silhouette. The class identity is tome, sigil, spirit focus, or controlled summoned energy, not direct weapon combat. Her build is petite and elegant, with a ghostly commander feel rather than a wild witch feel. The palette is cold teal, black-blue fabric, pale skin, and cyan spirit glow. Her signature is the long teal robe, spectral pale face, and small cyan spirit magic around the hands.
