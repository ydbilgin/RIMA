PROMPT WRITING RULE (HARD RULE):
Custom Animation V3 already knows the character (face, armor, color) from PixelLab.
BUT weapon position, grip, and movement arc must be explicitly written in every attack animation.

Every attack/weapon prompt must include:
1. Where the weapon starts (position, angle)
2. Grip (two-handed / one-handed / dominant hand)
3. Weapon movement (direction, arc -- "sweeps horizontally left", "drives downward", "thrusts forward")
4. Peak frame (most dramatic moment -- this is what AI anchors on)
5. Body mechanics (rotation direction, weight transfer, which shoulder/hip drives)
6. End position

For non-weapon animations (idle, hurt, death, dash): body mechanics + frame breakdown is sufficient.

Length: 50-120 words (attack), 30-60 words (idle/hurt/dash). Do NOT write short prompts.

CLASS WEAPON INFO:

Warblade -- Greatsword (two-handed):
- Grip: both hands on hilt, right hand dominant/top
- Blade: ~3.5 head-heights long, wide blade
- Rest pose: over right shoulder or hanging down on right side
- LMB: horizontal slash (left to right or right to left arc)
- RMB: vertical slam (overhead then straight down)

Ranger -- Compound bow (left hand):
- Left hand holds bow (bow grip), right hand draws string
- Draw position: left arm extended, right hand at cheek level (anchor point)
- LMB: quick shot (draw then release in one motion)
- RMB: slow aim shot (long hold draw, aiming)

Shadowblade -- Twin short blades (one in each hand):
- Left hand + right hand each hold a short blade
- Blade length: ~0.6x character height
- Reverse grip or forward grip (per design)
- LMB: right blade horizontal slash + left blade on guard
- RMB: left blade forward thrust / phase-strike

Elementalist -- No weapon (magic hand gestures):
- Hands free, fingers open/curled for casting
- Leave palm zone clear for VFX (~12-16px)
- No weapon -- write only hand position and arm mechanics
